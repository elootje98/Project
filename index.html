The effect of advertising bans and taxes on the percentage of smokers in a country.

Every year 7 million people die due to tabacco use. Smoking has harming effects on nearly every organ in the human body. Views on smoking are different all over the world, some religions even prohibit smoking. However, more and more people start to realize that smoking is extremely harmfull. Some governements take actions to protect their citizins against smoking. For example, banning tabacco advertisement. Also, there is said that rising taxes on tabacco, will make people less likely to smoke. On the other hand, critics say that raising taxes won't make a difference, but will only add an other problem: making tabacco addicts fall into poverty. In order to bring back the number of smokers, there needs to be clearification wether the current measures have effect. So does banning advertisement and raising taxes on tabacco, will make people smoke less? Click on the Data link to find out.
